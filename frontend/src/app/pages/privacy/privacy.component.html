ARMOR BRIDZ PRIVACY POLICY

When your organization uses Armor Bridz to run phishing simulations, you're trusting us with employee data. We take this responsibility seriously and are committed to protecting every piece of information.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect through the Armor Bridz phishing simulation platform, how we use it, how we protect it, and what rights you have regarding your data.

1. Introduction

Armor Bridz is a phishing simulation and security awareness platform that helps organizations assess employee readiness against phishing attacks. The platform involves sending simulated phishing emails, tracking employee interactions, and generating risk scores and compliance reports.

Because of the nature of our service, we handle employee information, simulation tracking data, and organizational data. This policy governs how all such data is collected, processed, stored, and shared.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data to operate the phishing simulation platform:

Administrator Data

  • Name, email address, and authentication credentials (managed through Firebase Authentication)
  • Account activity including campaign creation, template management, and report generation

Employee Data

  • First name, last name, and email address
  • Department, role (optional), and employment status (active/inactive)
  • Risk score — computed based on simulation interaction history
  • Last phishing test date

Simulation Tracking Data

  • Email delivery status (delivered/failed)
  • Email open events
  • Link click events with timestamp
  • Credential submission attempt flags (boolean only — see Section 4)
  • IP address (optional) and browser user agent

Campaign & Template Data

  • Campaign names, descriptions, target departments, and scheduling details
  • Phishing template content including subject lines, sender details, and HTML body
  • Campaign performance statistics (sent, delivered, opened, clicked, credential attempts)

3. How We Use Your Data

Data collected through the platform is used exclusively for:

  • Phishing simulation delivery — To send simulated phishing emails to enrolled employees and track their interactions
  • Risk assessment — To calculate per-employee and per-department risk scores based on simulation behavior (opened email = low risk, clicked link = medium risk, credential attempt = high risk)
  • Analytics & dashboards — To display campaign performance, department statistics, risk distribution, and trend data on the admin dashboard
  • Compliance reporting — To generate campaign summary reports, employee failure reports, and department risk reports in PDF and CSV formats for ISO 27001 readiness and security audits
  • Platform improvement — To improve the accuracy and effectiveness of our simulation engine and template library

4. Credential Safety — Our Core Promise

This is the most important section of our privacy policy. When employees interact with simulated phishing pages on Armor Bridz:

  • We NEVER store actual passwords. When an employee enters credentials on a fake login page, the system records only a boolean flag: credentialAttempted: true
  • No password data is captured, logged, transmitted, or stored at any point
  • The username/email field may be recorded solely to identify which employee triggered the event
  • This design is intentional and fundamental to the platform's architecture

This safeguard exists in both the frontend (phishing landing component) and the backend tracking service. There is no mechanism in the system capable of storing password data.

5. Data Storage & Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • Database: All data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, benefiting from Google's infrastructure-level security, encryption at rest, and automatic backups
  • Authentication: Admin access is secured through Firebase Authentication with JWT-based token verification on every API request
  • API Security: The Spring Boot backend uses Spring Security with a custom Firebase filter to validate all authenticated requests
  • Transport Security: All data in transit is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS
  • Access Control: Admin endpoints are protected; only the tracking log endpoint is publicly accessible (for phishing landing page interactions)

6. Data Sharing

We treat your data with strict confidentiality:

  • We never sell employee data, tracking data, or any organizational information to third parties
  • We do not share data with advertising networks, data brokers, or marketing platforms
  • Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud) is our sole infrastructure provider and processes data under Google's enterprise data processing terms
  • We may disclose data if required by law, regulation, or valid legal process
  • Aggregated, fully anonymized data may be used internally for platform performance analysis

7. Your Rights

As an organization administrator, you have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all employee records, campaign data, and tracking events through the admin dashboard
  • Export: Download campaign summaries, employee reports, and department risk data in CSV and PDF formats
  • Modification: Edit employee details, update campaign configurations, and modify templates at any time
  • Deletion: Remove employees, campaigns, and associated tracking data from the platform
  • Portability: Export all your data in standard formats for migration or compliance purposes

Employees enrolled in simulations may request access to their risk score and simulation history through their organization's administrator.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

The Armor Bridz platform uses minimal tracking technologies:

  • Session cookies: Required for authentication and maintaining your admin session
  • Firebase Auth tokens: JWT tokens stored in browser local storage for secure API access
  • Simulation tracking: Tracking pixels and unique link parameters are used in phishing simulation emails to detect opens and clicks — these are core to the platform's functionality

We do not use third-party advertising cookies, social media tracking pixels, or analytics tools that share data with external parties.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when:

  • New features are added to the phishing simulation platform (e.g., LMS integration, automated training assignment)
  • Our data handling practices evolve to meet new security standards
  • Legal or regulatory requirements change in the jurisdictions where our platform operates

Significant changes will be communicated through email or in-platform notifications. We encourage administrators to review this policy periodically.

This Privacy Policy governs how Armor Bridz handles data within the phishing simulation platform. Our commitment is simple: we collect only what's necessary, we never store passwords, and we never sell your data.

For privacy-related questions, data requests, or concerns, please contact our support team. Your trust is the foundation of our platform.
