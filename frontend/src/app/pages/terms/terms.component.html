2. Platform Purpose & Scope

Armor Bridz is an enterprise phishing simulation platform that enables organizations to:

  • Create and deploy simulated phishing campaigns to test employee awareness
  • Track employee interactions including email opens, link clicks, and credential submission attempts
  • Generate risk scores for individual employees and departments
  • Produce compliance reports for security audits and ISO 27001 readiness
  • Manage phishing email templates with customizable subjects, sender details, and HTML body content

The platform is intended exclusively for authorized security awareness testing within your own organization. It must not be used for malicious purposes, unauthorized access attempts, or targeting individuals outside your organization.

3. Account & Access Control

Access to the Armor Bridz admin panel requires authentication through Firebase Authentication. As an administrator, you are responsible for:

  • Maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials
  • All activity that occurs under your account, including campaign creation and employee management
  • Ensuring that only authorized personnel within your organization have admin access
  • Reporting any unauthorized access or security breaches immediately

Employee accounts are managed by administrators. Employees are enrolled in simulations through manual entry or CSV upload and do not require separate login credentials to participate in phishing simulations.

Violation of this acceptable use policy may result in immediate account suspension or termination, and may be reported to relevant authorities.

5. Simulated Phishing Campaigns

Armor Bridz enables administrators to create phishing simulation campaigns that may include:

  • Simulated phishing emails sent to employee inboxes using customizable templates
  • Fake login pages designed to detect credential submission attempts
  • Tracking mechanisms that record email delivery, opens, link clicks, and form submissions

Important safety guarantee: Armor Bridz does not store actual employee passwords or credentials. When an employee submits credentials on a simulated login page, the system records only a boolean flag indicating that a credential attempt was detected. No password data is captured, transmitted, or stored.

Organizations are responsible for informing employees about the existence of phishing simulation programs as part of their security awareness policy.

6. Data Handling & Privacy

Armor Bridz collects and processes the following data to operate the platform:

  • Employee information: name, email address, department, role, and status
  • Campaign data: template content, target groups, scheduling, and delivery status
  • Tracking events: email opens, link clicks, credential attempt flags, timestamps, and optional IP addresses
  • Risk scores: computed per-employee based on simulation interaction history

All data is stored in Firebase Firestore and transmitted securely. For full details on how we handle your data, please review our Privacy Policy.
